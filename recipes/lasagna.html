Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish of Italian origin, made up of layers of thin fresh pasta, filling (which can be bolognese, cheese, etc.) and sauce. It is a very versatile dish, and can be adapted to suit different tastes and ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 lasagna noodles
  • 500g mozzarella cheese
  • tomato paste
  • black pepper to taste
  • 500g ham
  • 500g minced meat
  • salt to taste
  1. Prepare the bolognese sauce
    • In a big pan, heat a drizzle of olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic until golden
    • Add the ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon
    • Stir in the copped peeled tomatoes, tomato sauce, water, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer over low heat for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens.
  2. Assemble the lasagna:
    • Preheat the oven to 180°C(350°F)
    • In a rectangular baking dish, spread a thin layer of Bolognese sauce on the bottom
    • Cover with a layer of lasagna sheets
    • Spread a layer of Bolognese sauce over the pasta, followed by slices of ham and slices of mozzarella
    • Repeat the layers (sauce, pasta, sauce, ham, mozzarella) until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of Bolognese sauce and mozzarella on top.
    • If desired, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the top.
  3. Bake
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted, bubbly, and lightly browned.
  4. Serve
    • Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
